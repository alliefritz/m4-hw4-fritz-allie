My Favorite Artist: Bring Me The Horizon

This is a webpage dedicated to the English rock band Bring Me The Horizon.

About the Band

Bring Me The Horizon was formed in 2004 in Sheffield, England, and features the following band members:

  • Oli Sykes, lead singer, keyboards, additional studio guitars
  • Lee Malia, lead guitar and rhythm guitar
  • Matt Kean, bass
  • Matt Nicholls, drums
  • Jordan Fish, keyboards, programming, drum pads, percussion, backing vocals

The band's name is a play on the iconic quote from the movie Pirates of the Caribbean: Curse of the Black Pearl, where Johnny Depp's character, Captain Jack Sparrow, says, "Now bring me that horizon."

Discography

Evolution of Their Sound

While the band started out as a hardcore, metalcore-focused band, over time, their sound has evolved and expanded as they have experimented with different sounds and styles, while keeping the integrity of what the band stands for. The band saw some pushback from older fans who were upset the sound has strayed from its darker metalcore roots, and the band has addressed that in the song "Heavy Metal" off their 2019 album, Amo.

List of Bring Me The Horizon's Discography:

  • There Is a Hell Believe Me I've Seen It. There Is a Heaven Let's Keep It a Secret (2010)

  • Post Human: Survival Horror (2020)
  • Post Human: Next Gen (2023, coming soon)

Why This Band

I chose Bring Me The Horizon for this assignment because, while I have a lot of favorite musical artists, this is an incredibly special band to me. I have loved this band for 14 years, over half of my lifetime. The care, thought, effort, and love this entire band puts into their music is evident. I was recently able to see this band live for the first time and they hold a special place in my heart.
